A hidden gem in the city...

Lan's Beauty Barber Shop is perfectly located in First Hill in downtown Seattle. This hole in the wall location is great for a quick stop. Lan has been in the community for decades helping locals look and feel their best with a fresh hair cut or style. If you want a more personalized experience that will leave you looking and feeling amazing, come on by! Or, if you would like, we are more than happy to schedule an appointment for when its convenient for you. Hope to see you soon!
