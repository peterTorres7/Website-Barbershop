About us!

We are a small, family owned, business in a big city. Seattle has been home to us for many years and with the loyal customers we have accumulated, we hope to be in your neighborhood for a long time. Lan's Beauty Barber Shop is mainly run by Lan herself. Her detailed and quick, service is what she is known for. This personalized service is at the core of our beliefs. We believe that you are an individual and should be treated as such. No two haircuts are the same! Making sure that you are taken care off and leave looking your best is what we do best. Feel free to walk-in or schedule an appointment at your convenience. Look foward to providing you with excellent service!
